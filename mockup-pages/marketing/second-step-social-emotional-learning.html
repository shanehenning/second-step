Who is Second Step for?

Our program is suitable for districts, schools, and communities of any size, and in any location. We developed stand-alone, age-appropriate programs for Early Learning, Elementary, and Middle School classrooms.

Why Choose the Second Step SEL Program?

Trusted By Schools

Second Step has been successfully implemented by 26,000+ schools worldwide. Our curriculum is designed to align with district standards.

Easy To Use

Educators love using Second Step because lesson plans and activities are easy to implement and require little prep time.

Engaging and Useful

Students eagerly anticipate Second Step's fun, captivating lessons that include games, activities, and songs. The skills they learn are useful in school, on the playground, and at home.

Endorsed

Second Step has been endorsed by numerous organizations, including Collaborative for Academic, Social, and Emotional Learning (CASEL) and the Department of Education, for the quality of our research, training, and teacher and family support.
